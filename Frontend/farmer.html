🐛 Pests & Remedies ▼
🦟
Aphids (Mahu)
Symptom: Yellowing leaves, sticky honeydew on shoots
✅ Remedy: Spray Imidacloprid 17.8% SL @ 0.5 ml/L water. Use neem oil as organic alternative.
🍄
Yellow Rust (Peela Ratua)
Symptom: Yellow stripe pustules on leaves, severe yield loss
✅ Remedy: Spray Propiconazole 25% EC @ 1 ml/L at first sign. Resistant varieties: HD-2781.
🐌
Termites (Deemak)
Symptom: Wilting, hollow stems, roots eaten at soil level
✅ Remedy: Seed treatment with Chlorpyrifos 20 EC @ 5 ml/kg seed before sowing.
